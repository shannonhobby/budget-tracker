ACCOUNTING PRINCIPLES

50/30/20 RULE:

Approximately 50% of spending should go towards living expenses.
Approximately 30% of spending should be allotted to discretionary spending.
Approximately 20% of spending should go towards savings and paying off debts.

HOW CAN THIS RULE WORK IN PRACTICE?

-Users should be alerted (Note: pop-up?) when their proportion of these categories is statistically significant (Note: 5-10% difference).
